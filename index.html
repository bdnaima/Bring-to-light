Counselling Services

SERVICES

I offer counselling services in a space free from judgements. Whether you are experiencing anxiety, low self-esteem, depression, relationship problems, thoughts of self-harm, having difficulty coping with day-to-day life, experienced trauma or other concerns not listed here, you are welcomed. Together we can help bring to light what is going on and explore some possible solutions.

SOMATIC THERAPY

I am a certified "Mindfulness-based Somatic Therapist" and "Craniosacral Therapist" level 1. There are times when talking about our problems does not seem enough. This is when the problem seems deeper-rooted within us and locks into our "soma" meaning body. By following our felt sense we may release "stuck" emotions through this experience.

ABOUT ME

Hey there! I'm Amnah and am a Registered Counsellor (RTCc) with ACCT. I am Certified in Mindfulness-based Somatic Therapy as well as LVL 1 Craniosacral Therapy. I have experience counselling individuals facing grief, anxiety, depression, parenting and relationship concerns, self-esteem, trauma and more. 

Beyond by profession

My family background is South Asian, however, I was raised in Sweden. As an adult, I moved to Canada to start my family. This has given me the wonderful gift of knowledge about different cultures, values, traditions and languages.

Naturally, I have listened to people sharing their stories with great interest and care. This has led me to build a deep understanding and compassion for the human experience that is unique to everyone.

When faced with my own challenges in life I was, with the right support, guidance, self-education, able to find coping skills to see the light out of darkness to support my life.

Today I find it an honour to be able to potentially be that right support to you and help you on your unique and personal journeys.
