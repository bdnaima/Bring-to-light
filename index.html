About Amnah Malik

Coming from an East Indian heritage, being raised in Sweden, living in Canada for over a decade has blessed me with the wonderful opportunity to experience different cultures and meet people from different walks of life.

Naturally, I have listened to people sharing their stories with great interest and care. This has led me to build a deep understanding and compassion for the human experience that is unique to everyone.

When faced with my own challenges in life I was, with the right support, guidance, self-education, able to find coping skills to see the light out of the darkness.

I now want to devote my time helping others find their own unique paths.

This is what has shaped my counselling practice.

I want to welcome you in to this team effort experience between one human to another, not just a counsellor to client.

Contact me to find out more or book an appointment.
