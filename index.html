About Amnah Malik

Coming from an Asian upbringing, raised in Sweden(21 years) and living over 10 years in Canada has given me a wonderful oppurtunity to learn about different cultures and meet people from all walks of life. By always offering a listening ear, I have learned to have a deep understanding and compassion for others. I have always had an urge to help people but didn't always know how to.

When I faced challenges of my own I was, with help able to find solutions and learn many coping strategies that worked for me. That's when I decided to devote my time helping others find their solutions to the unique situation.

I have completed my Diploma in "Professional counselling" from Vancouver College of Counselling training. I also volunteer my time, facilitating art therapy class. Currently, I am only offering individual online video counselling or over the phone counselling.

If you would like to know more about me and services or would like a free 20 minute consultation, feel free to contact me.

I am here to help.
