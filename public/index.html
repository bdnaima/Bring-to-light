Counselling Services

COUNSELLING

Let’s be honest. Life is sometimes hard, confusing and stressful. Although it is a normal part of life, there are times when the problem seems to reoccur or last longer than is healthy for us.

Speaking with a counsellor / therapist is proven to be effective in easing stress, building resilience and learning ways to cope.

More Info

SOMATIC THERAPY

My specialization is in Mindfulness-Based Somatic Therapy®. Trauma and stress have a way of becoming locked in our bodies, showing up as undesired symptoms.

Somatic therapy takes you on a very personal journey using restorative yoga postures, breathing techniques, mindfulness and resourcing practices, movement and use of “felt sense” to release and heal.

Hi! I'm Amnah.

I am a Registered Therapeutic Counsellor (RTC) with ACCT with four years of experiance in counselling individuals facing greif, anxiety, depression, parenting and relationship concerns, low self-esteem, trauma, and more. When supporting clients I draw from various modalities such as Motivational Interviewing, CBT, Mindfulness, Somatic therapy and am Certified in Mindfulness-Based Somatic Therapy®. However, the most important thing for me is building a trusting relaitionship and a proving a safe and space to explore thoughts, feelings, and emotions free from judgement.

Beyond my profession
I was born in Pakistan and raised in Sweden. As an adult, I moved to Canada to start my family. This has given me the wonderful gift of knowledge about different cultures, values, traditions and languages.

Naturally, I have listened to people sharing their stories with great interest and care. This has led me to build a deep understanding and compassion for the human experience that is unique to everyone.

When faced with my own challenges in life I was, with the right support, guidance, self-education, able to find coping skills to see the light out of darkness to support my life.

Today I find it an honour to be able to potentially be that right support to you and help you on your unique and personal journey.

Warmest,
Amnah Malik
